From Performer to Person: My Reclamation Journey

For years, I wore competence like armor - until the weight of performance crushed my spirit. My journey back to self-belonging began when I realized that surviving wasn't the same as living.

Survival

Building walls to protect a tender heart, learning to perform safety instead of feeling it.

Burnout

When the mask became too heavy and the performance too exhausting to maintain.

Realization

The moment I understood that my greatest strength was also my greatest prison.

Reclamation

Rising from the ashes of who I thought I should be into who I truly am.
